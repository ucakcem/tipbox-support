TipBox

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

TipBox ("the app") is a restaurant tip planning app for iOS. It helps you distribute tips among staff using different models (equal split, hours-based, role-based, point system, sales-based, tip pool). We do not collect, store, or share your personal data on our servers. Your information stays on your device or in your own Apple account.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the data you enter—such as employee names and roles, tip sessions, distribution settings, and preferences (currency, language)—locally on your iPhone or iPad. This data is not sent to us or any third party for our own use.

In-App Purchases

TipBox Pro subscriptions are processed by Apple. We do not handle or store your payment details. Apple's payment and privacy terms apply. You can manage subscriptions in your device Settings under Apple ID → Subscriptions.

Analytics & Advertising

We do not use third-party analytics or advertising in the app. We do not track your usage for marketing purposes.

Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at ucak@outlook.com.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will be revised when we do. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
